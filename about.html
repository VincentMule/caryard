What we are:

Founded in 2012, Smileymotors Limited is one of Kenya's respected automotive dealers. We pride in offering quality cars and exceptional service.

Our Mission:

Buying a vehicle is not only about price, but also quality and service. Buying a car is a major purchase, it is therefore very important that one is confident they are dealing with an honest and reputable dealer. With us, making a sale is not only about making business, but also building relations.

Our Vision:

Smileymotors has changed the way you feel about shopping for a vehicle. You will find that we do not pressurize; we offer advice, and if we have not got what you want then we will endeavor to fulfill your requirements. Customer satisfaction is key.
